E Bot is a fun and interactive Discord bot designed to keep your server lively and engaging! Here’s what it does:

  • 💥 Tracks “E” messages in a dedicated channel and automatically updates a leaderboard.
  • 🗑️ Deletes non-“E” messages in the E channel to keep it clean.
  • ⚡ Instant leaderboard updates with top participants shown in real-time.
  • 🔧 Easy server setup with just one slash command to create the necessary channels.
  • 🎖️ Future role rewards and premium features to make your server even more fun.
